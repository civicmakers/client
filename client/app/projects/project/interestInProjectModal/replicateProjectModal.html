Bring This Project To My City

Thanks for your interest in bringing this project to your city! You can get started by:

1) Ask the project owner how you can help. Add your question to the project page and we’ll notify the project owner about your message.

2) Contact us and one of our team will email you to talk about where you are at and what you’d like to do.

3) Download this guide to civic projects to learn about the steps to starting and finishing your project.
